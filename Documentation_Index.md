## FAQ

**Q: Where do I start?**
A: Begin with [ISSUES_RESOLVED.md](./ISSUES_RESOLVED.md) for a complete overview.

**Q: How do I verify the fixes work?**
A: Run `bash check-adsense-compliance.sh` or test locally with `npm start`.

**Q: What's the 300-word requirement?**
A: Google AdSense requires at least 300 words of meaningful content on pages with ads.

**Q: Do I need to update anything?**
A: Yes, update `.env` with your AdSense Client ID before production deployment.

**Q: Is this production-ready?**
A: Yes! All code is tested and documented. Ready to deploy immediately.

**Q: Will this break my site?**
A: No. Changes are backward compatible and improve user experience.

**Q: How long until Google reviews this?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours for initial policy review.
